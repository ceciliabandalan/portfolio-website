About

Hi, I'm Cecilia

I am an ENFJ who is often described by friends and Family as diligent and structured, with a playful sense of humor. Naturally, I am empathetic and curious, with a passion for learning more about the human experience.

Like most designers, UX/UI sorta just…called me, mainly because, to me, meaningful work involves enriching the lives of others. This field allows me to combine my creative and analytical expertise into a career that’s obsessed with the user.

Off the clock, you’ll find me wandering into tiny shops around San Francisco, strolling along the beaches of Half Moon Bay, or in my kitchen meticulously prepping for another dinner party.
